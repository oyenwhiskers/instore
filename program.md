## Essential (RM450 / month)
**Purpose:** Small-scale events with basic execution and KPI visibility, with strict limits.

### Limits
- Automatic product data processing: **Up to 10 products**
- Multi-location / outlet management: **Up to 3 outlets**
- Dashboards: **1 dashboard view**
- Customer tracking: **Up to 5 customers**

### Inventory
- **Basic inventory tracking**

### Included Core Features (Baseline)
- Engagement tracking
- Alerts & notifications
- Multi-language interface
- Export & share reports / analysis
- Centralized event & promoter management
- Real-time KPI & custom KPI
- Photo check-in / check-out
- Sampling & giveaway tracking
- Event calendar & scheduling
- Historical & growth analysis

---

## Advanced (RM980 / month)
**Purpose:** Mid-scale operations requiring better control, reporting, and team visibility.

### Limits
- Automatic product data processing: **Up to 30 products**
- Multi-location / outlet management: **Unlimited**
- Dashboards: **Management dashboard + Promoter dashboard**
- Customer tracking: **Up to 20 customers**

### Inventory
- **Full inventory management**
  - Detailed tracking
  - Inventory reporting

### Included Core Features
- All **Essential** features
- Enhanced inventory control
- Role-based dashboard separation (management vs promoter)

---

## Ultimate (RM1,750 / month)
**Purpose:** Large agencies or brands managing multiple events with multi-stakeholder reporting and no caps.

### Limits
- Automatic product data processing: **Unlimited**
- Multi-location / outlet management: **Unlimited**
- Dashboards: **4 dashboard views**
  - Brand Client
  - Manager
  - Supervisor
  - Promoter
- Customer tracking: **Unlimited**

### Inventory
- **Full inventory management**
  - Detailed tracking
  - Advanced reporting

### Included Core Features
- All **Advanced** features
- Multi-stakeholder dashboard access
- No operational limits
- Enterprise-scale reporting & analytics

---

## Tier Differentiation Summary
- **Essential:** Entry-level with strict limits and single dashboard
- **Advanced:** Removes outlet limits, adds full inventory and role-based dashboards
- **Ultimate:** Removes all limits and adds brand/client/supervisor-level visibility
